Methodology & Metric Definitions

Risk is calculated as a composite index of conflict pressure and systemic fragility. Only schools with a Final Score > 0.7 are displayed in this report.

  • Conflict Score: Measures historical and recent conflict events within a 10km radius of the school (0–1 scale).
  • Fragility Uplift: Adjusts the risk based on regional education baseline indicators, such as out-of-school rates and survival rates (0–1 scale).
  • Final Score: The combined composite vulnerability index used to categorize schools.
